About Us and Why us

Shruthi & Associatesis a Registered Firm of practicing Chartered Accountants registered with The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (under The Act of Parliament). The firm bears the names of its founder partners, Venugopal Gella and Vinay Thyagaraj. Firm started its operation in 2002 with Bangalore Office. Now the firm has expanded its reach by adding branch offices at Chennai, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Ananthapur and Vishakapatnam. The firm has a a strong and dedicated force of 16 qualified personnel (which includes 10 partners), having CA's /CS's / CWA's / MBA's and LLB's as qualifications.
